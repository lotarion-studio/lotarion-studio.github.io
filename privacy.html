LOTARION

Privacy Policy — All Games & Applications

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all games and applications developed and published by Lotarion ("we", "us", or "our"), including but not limited to GemBlast and any future titles. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our games are designed with privacy in mind. By default, we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Name, email address, or contact details
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Photos, contacts, or other device content

3. Local Data Storage & Data We May Collect

Our applications store certain preferences locally on your device (such as language selection, sound settings, and game progress) using your device's local storage. This data remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Some features, where available, may involve transmitting limited data to third-party services:

  • Leaderboards: If you participate in leaderboards, your in-game username and score may be transmitted to and stored by Google Play Games Services. This data is used solely to display rankings and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Some of our applications may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — used for typography. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play — app distribution platform. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

If future applications integrate advertising or analytics SDKs, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for general audiences including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no personal data, our applications are safe for users of all ages.

6. Permissions

Some applications may request device permissions (such as vibration). These permissions are used solely to enhance gameplay experience and do not involve collecting or transmitting any data.

7. Future Updates to Our Services

As Lotarion grows and new features are introduced (such as leaderboards, cloud saves, or advertising), this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via the contact email listed on our Google Play developer page.
